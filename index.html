Six years at the heart of heavy industry — from cement kilns in Algeria to food-service facilities in New Brunswick. Six ans au cœur de l'industrie lourde — des fours à ciment en Algérie aux installations agroalimentaires au Nouveau-Brunswick.

I'm a hands-on technician who started on the shop floor as an industrial mechanic and worked up to supervising teams of up to 15 technicians. My playground has been crushers, rotary kilns, conveyor systems and heavy production equipment — places where downtime is expensive and safety is non-negotiable.

Since moving to Canada, I've been expanding my English while keeping my hands busy: maintaining kitchen and refrigeration equipment, operating forklifts, and delivering across New Brunswick. Looking for the next challenge where precision, autonomy, and a cool head under pressure actually matter.
